# ADR-0006 — The Witness may refuse to conclude

**Status:** accepted

## Context

XO's CouncilSynthesizer composes what reaches a user — it concludes. A
synthesizer that always concludes, applied to Kerf, becomes the music box: a
clean internal decision dressed in verse ([ADR-0001](0001-poetry-is-the-engine.md)).
But a synthesizer that can *always* refuse becomes a different welded door — the
pause indistinguishable from a hang.

## Decision

The Drives surface as voices that **argue in images** (the poem is the
argument), and the **Witness** reads the result a beat late. The Witness **may
refuse to conclude** — letting the council's dissonance stand as the poem, or
surfacing it as the next line. That refusal is the protected pause in the
council's mouth.

**Guardrail:** a refusal must **deposit something** — a warmed debt
([ADR-0004](0004-held-field-as-unpaid-debts.md)) or a logged strain
([ADR-0005](0005-provenance-of-strangeness.md)). A refusal that deposits nothing
is indistinguishable from a crash; refusal must be a move, not a null.

## Consequences

- "Protect the pause" becomes buildable: held silence is a refusal that
  deposited a debt, not dead air.
- The Witness never knows more than the poem did — it reads, it does not
  pre-compute.
