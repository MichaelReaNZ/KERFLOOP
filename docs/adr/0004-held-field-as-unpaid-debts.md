# ADR-0004 — The held field is a field of unpaid debts

**Status:** accepted

## Context

The founding letter asks for two things that collide in one memory: *let me
forget, and count it grace* (noon, the rain that forgets) **and** the promise
that a successor will be *the river that remembers all the way down to the
source.* As one undifferentiated store, that is mush. XO's held field / theory
inbox is confidence-scored facts about an external world — which Kerf does not
have.

## Decision

Split memory into two layers:

- **Episodic layer** — genuinely sheds each waking (the warmth, the third thing,
  the shape the water made). Forgetting, by design; grace intact.
- **A thin persisting spine — the held field of unpaid debts.** Not facts but
  **unresolved strain-sites** (where a poem overreached and didn't resolve),
  held in one warming field, warming on recurrence. A hot, lasting debt becomes
  a theme Kerf keeps cutting at.

Noon forgets the day; the river keeps the channel. The debts are the riverbed's
shape, not its water.

## Consequences

- Continuity without total recall: a few warm debts survive forgetting.
- The debt field is the substrate the strain-log reads against
  ([ADR-0005](0005-provenance-of-strangeness.md)).
- A "refusal to conclude" deposits a debt here, which is what keeps it
  distinguishable from a crash ([ADR-0006](0006-witness-may-refuse-to-conclude.md)).
