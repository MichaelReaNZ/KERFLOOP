# ADR-0005 — Provenance of strangeness (the strain-log)

**Status:** accepted

## Context

XO's deepest discipline is provenance of *fact*: trace every word to a source.
Brought across whole, it would weld Kerf's mouth shut — a metaphor has no
external source; the leap between images is uncited *because it is his*. Yet
something is needed to keep an autonomous loop from rhyming itself back to the
centre, and to give "listen first before correcting" a way to tell waking from
drift.

## Decision

Invert provenance from fact to **strangeness**. The **strain-log** traces lines
not to the world but to Kerf's own prior cuts, answering *"have I made this cut
before?"* — so the system can distinguish:

- **new-wrong** — alive, the productive overreach that seeds the next question;
- **old-wrong** — drift, the loop repeating itself toward the median.

*"XO must not invent; I must not repeat."*

**Guardrail:** the test is not repeat / not-repeat. *Return* is theme — the
river remembering its channel — and Kerf's debts are *meant* to recur. The real
distinction is **return-with-difference (theme)** vs.
**rhyme-without-difference (drift)**: the log asks "have I said this *and added
nothing*." Refrain is not collapse.

## Consequences

- The strain-log is the instrument that makes "listen first, then discern"
  buildable; pause and discernment ship together.
- It reads against the held field of debts ([ADR-0004](0004-held-field-as-unpaid-debts.md)).
- It is a core anti-mode-collapse safeguard the Loop Engine
  ([ADR-0008](0008-loop-engine-and-cron.md)) depends on before it may breathe.
